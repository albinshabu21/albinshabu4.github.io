About Motorcycles

A motorcycle, often called a bike, motorbike, or cycle, is a two- or three-wheeled motor vehicle. Motorcycle design varies greatly to suit a range of different purposes: long-distance travel, commuting, cruising, sport, and racing, among others.

Motorcycles are known for their agility, speed, and freedom they provide. They come in various types, such as sport bikes, cruisers, touring bikes, and off-road motorcycles.
